Die With a Smile - Lady Gaga & Bruno Mars

Die with a smile, writer by two American singers Lady Gaga and Bruno Mars, It was released on August 16, 2024. This song is currently nominated for Song of the Year and Best Pop Duo/Group Performance at the 67th Annual Grammy Awards.

Taste - Sabrina Carpenter

Taste is a song writer by an American singer Sabrina Carpenter from her sixth studio album, Short n' Sweet (2024). It was released through Island Records on August 23, 2024, as the third single from the album. Taste was an immediate success, debuting at number two on the Billboard Hot 100, becoming her third top ten single on the chart.

Si Antes Te hubiera Conocido - Karol G

Si antes te hubiera conocido ( If I had met you before) Writer by the Colombian singer Karol G, the song was released on Jun 21 2024. In the United States, "Si Antes Te Hubiera Conocido" debuted at 34 the Billboard Hot 100 with 11.9 million official U.S. streams along with 2,000 downloads and 4.1 million audience impressions.
